Available commands:
    add_black_list_customer
        Adds customer to black list database.
    add_product
        Adds product to products database.
    remove_black_list_customer
        Removes customer from black list database.
    remove_product
        Removes product from products database.
    save_changes
        Saves all changes to databases.
    show_black_list
        Prints black list database.
    show_customers
        Prints customers database.
    show_products
        Print products database.
    quit
        Stops application.
